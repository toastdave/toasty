# Product Vision

## Working title

Toasty is a fun-but-serious media discovery app that helps people find what to watch, rate it in a more expressive way than five stars, and eventually battle through yearly tournament brackets.

## Product pillars

1. Useful in under ten seconds.
2. Playful enough to invite repeat browsing.
3. Deep enough to support long-term collecting, rating, and competition.

## Launch posture

The launch surface is anime-first because it gives us a clear source strategy through Jikan, a passionate user loop, and high-value discovery pages with relatively low operational overhead.

## MVP outcome

The first release should let a new visitor:

- browse top anime all time
- browse current airing schedules
- inspect a detail page with canonical metadata and source links
- understand season progress at a glance when the source data supports it
- see the shape of the future product through ratings, lists, and tournament affordances

## Long-range expansion

Once the anime vertical is healthy, the data model and service layer should expand into movies, TV, books, and music without forcing a UI rewrite.
