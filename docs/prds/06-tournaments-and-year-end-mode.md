# Tournaments And Year End Mode

## Why tournaments exist

The long-term differentiator is turning annual media discourse into a playful, competitive bracket season.

## Seeding model

Yearly rankings should blend:

- aggregate community rating
- popularity and trend performance
- engagement volume
- limited editorial or cult-hit boosts

## User promise

When tournaments ship, users should be able to:

- browse seeded brackets
- vote in active matchups
- debate upsets and favorites
- revisit historical winners without retroactive changes

## Release strategy

Tournament tables can exist early in the schema, but UX should wait until discovery, ratings, and profiles have enough real usage to seed meaningful brackets.
